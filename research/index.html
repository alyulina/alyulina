Linkage equilibrium between rare alleles

Together with Zhiru Liu and Benjamin Good, I am studying the out-of-linkage-equilibrium dynamics and their implications for bacterial recombination.


Population genomics of the spoon-billed sandpiper

I am using simulations and mathematical modeling to learn how past changes in the population size of a critically endangered bird species contributed to its present-day genetic diversity. In collaboration with Raimundo Saona, Mateusz Konczal, and Fyodor Kondrashov.
